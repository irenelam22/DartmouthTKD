About Us

Dartmouth Tae Kwon Do is a student-run organization that was founded in 1984. Presently, Dartmouth TKD practices the official South Korean and now Olympic (IOC) recognized form of Tae Kwon Do, the Kukkiwon World Taekwondo Federation (WTF) style, employing full contact and "modern training methods". We compete in the Eastern Collegiate Taekwondo Conference (ECTC) and perform demonstrations at events such as Dartmouth's annual Korean Culture Night.
