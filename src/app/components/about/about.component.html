About Us

Dartmouth Tae Kwon Do is a student-run organization that was founded in 1984. Presently, Dartmouth TKD practices the official South Korean and now Olympic (IOC) recognized form of Tae Kwon Do, the Kukkiwon World Taekwondo Federation (WTF) style.

We compete in the Eastern Collegiate Taekwondo Conference (ECTC) every term for both sparring and poomsae.

Fun fact: Brothers Sung Chul Whang and Jun Chul Whang, the founders of the Dartmouth Taekwondo Team, began the first ECTC match in the winter of 1983 when they competed against Yale's Taekwondo team and Princeton's Tangsoodo team on a weekend trip! More details of this can be found here.

We also perform demonstrations at events such as Dartmouth's annual Korean Culture Night. Previous performances have included kpop performances, wood breaking, coordinated sparring matches, blindfold breaking, and more!

Everyone is welcome, beginners included! For more information, check out faq page!
